form and perception @gcadd

This exhibition brings together a cross section of work from the studio creating a snapshot of time. Included is video vignettes of significant landscapes, digitally developed ceramic arrangements and visual studies in color and form. From first glance the work might appear disparate, but they gently echo one another through their form, sense of stillness and attention to spatial arrangement. Rather than attempting to tell a single narrative, this work invites you to move between pieces to create moments of quiet.

GCADD, Granite City, Illinois. August - November 2025.
